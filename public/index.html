Section 12

Aftermath · Damage & Medicals

Injuries sustained · NSAC medical suspensions · The biomechanical evidence
Kamaru Usman
  • Left thumb injury. Likely from right-hand impact in R5 finishing sequence. Required X-ray clearance.
  • Eye poke absorbed (R3 14:15). Covington's fingers extended during a kick-jab combination. Cleared by Octagonside physician.
  • Brief wobble absorbed in R4. Covington's three-punch combination staggered legs; no knockdown.
  • Facial blood. Cuts minor. No stitches documented.
  • Medical suspension: 180 days or until thumb X-ray cleared. Minimum 30 days no contact.
Colby Covington
  • Non-displaced midline mandible fracture. Confirmed by UFC medical staff and NSAC. Most likely caused by Usman's right cross in the final 1–2 min of R3. Dana White confirmed at post-fight presser.
  • Corner audio captured: "I think I broke my jaw." Broadcast live on ESPN between R3 and R4.
  • Two knockdowns in R5. Both from Usman's right hand to the (already fractured) jaw.
  • 14 ground strikes absorbed in 11 seconds before TKO stoppage.
  • Hospital transport post-fight. Full facial evaluation. Bloodied, swollen face.
  • Medical suspension: 180 days or until mandibular fracture cleared by oral & maxillofacial surgeon. Min 60 days no contact.
  • Resilience note: Fought ~10 minutes (R4 + R5) with a broken jaw · absorbed 59 head strikes post-fracture · matched Usman's output 36-to-35 in R4.
